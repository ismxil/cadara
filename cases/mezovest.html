Mezovest

A platform foundation for better business credit.

Background

Businesses cannot meet the high collateral conditions from traditional banks, which forces them to source credit from loans sharks that are charging ridiculous monthly interest and not helping their business in any way to pay back the loan.


Mezovest directly addresses these inefficiencies in the credit value chain by providing businesses a platform and tools that will allow them seamless manage their day to day business activities, give them loans and make paying back more convenient.

Solution
